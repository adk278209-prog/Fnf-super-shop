### Step 3: Competitive Pricing Strategy

**Your advantage is COST, not price matching:**

```
EXAMPLE: Rupchanda Soyabean Oil 5L

Retail Price (Market):          600 BDT
├─ Chaldal Price:              550 BDT
├─ Shwapno Price:              560 BDT
└─ Your Cost (bulk):            280 BDT

Your Pricing:
├─ Chaldal match:              550 BDT (20% margin, good for market share)
├─ Your profit:                270 BDT (96% higher than Chaldal!)
└─ Still undercuts some:        520 BDT (if you want aggressive growth)

Why you can undercut them:
├─ Bulk buying directly:        -10%
├─ No Dhaka logistics:          -5%
├─ Lower admin overhead:        -3%
└─ No need for venture funding ROI: Can go lower
```

**Pricing Framework:**

```
Your Cost + (Your Cost × Markup %) = Your Price

Category Markup Guidelines:
├─ Essential (oil, rice, flour):    35-45% markup (low margin, high volume)
├─ Grocery items:                   50-60% markup
├─ Personal care:                   65-75% markup (higher margin)
└─ Specialty items:                 80-100% markup
```
